# Products FAQ

## Sizing
- Size guide available on every product page
- When between sizes, we recommend sizing up
- Fit notes are included in product descriptions
- Free exchanges if size doesn't fit

## Product Care
- Care instructions are on the label and product page
- General: wash cold, hang dry for longest life
- Wool items: hand wash or dry clean only
- Waterproof items: do not tumble dry

## Stock & Availability
- "Notify Me" button available on out-of-stock items
- Restocks typically happen within 2-4 weeks
- Limited edition items are not restocked
- Pre-orders ship on the listed release date

## Warranties
- All products have a 1-year warranty against defects
- Electronics have a 2-year warranty
- Warranty does not cover normal wear and tear
- To claim warranty: contact support with order number and photos
