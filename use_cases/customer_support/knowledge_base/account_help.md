# Account Help

## Login Issues
- Reset password at: myaccount.example.com/reset
- Password must be 8+ characters with one number and one symbol
- After 5 failed attempts, account locks for 30 minutes
- If still locked after 30 minutes, contact support

## Managing Your Account
- Update email: Account Settings > Personal Info
- Update payment: Account Settings > Payment Methods
- Update address: Account Settings > Addresses
- Delete account: Contact support (takes 30 days to process)

## Loyalty Programme
- Earn 1 point per £1 spent
- 100 points = £5 reward
- Points expire after 12 months of inactivity
- Silver tier: 500+ points/year (free express shipping)
- Gold tier: 1000+ points/year (early access + free next day shipping)

## Payment Methods
- We accept: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Klarna available for orders over £30 (pay in 3 installments)
- Gift cards can be combined with other payment methods
