# Shipping Information

## Delivery Options
| Method | Timeframe | Cost |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Standard | 5-7 business days | Free over £50, otherwise £4.99 |
| Express | 2-3 business days | £9.99 |
| Next Day | Next business day (order by 2pm) | £14.99 |
| International | 10-15 business days | From £19.99 |

## Tracking
- All orders include tracking
- Tracking emails are sent within 24 hours of dispatch
- Track at: tracking.example.com
- If tracking hasn't updated in 3+ days, contact us

## Delivery Issues
- Missing package: wait 48 hours after estimated delivery, then contact us
- Damaged package: take photos and contact us within 48 hours
- Wrong item: contact us immediately for a free replacement
- Partial order: some items may ship separately

## Free Shipping
- Free standard shipping on all orders over £50
- Free shipping on all exchange orders
- Loyalty members get free express shipping
